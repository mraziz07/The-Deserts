What is Desert?

Defination:

A desert is a barren and arid landscape characterized by a scarcity of precipitation, resulting in minimal vegetation. Deserts can be found on every continent and are known for their unique ecosystems adapted to survive in harsh environmental conditions, including places with extreme temperatures, both hot and cold. This includes regions like Antarctica, which, despite its frigid temperatures, is considered a cold desert due to its low precipitation levels.
